Description: `dagster-slurm at SciPy 2026 — from laptop to supercomputer without rewriting your pipeline`

import useBaseUrl from '@docusaurus/useBaseUrl';

Accepted talk at [SciPy 2026](https://www.scipy2026.scipy.org/). Co-presented by
[Georg Heiler](https://georgheiler.com/) and
[Hernan Picatto](https://ascii.ac.at/person/hernan-picatto/) (ASCII).

The deck focuses on the laptop → HPC path and spends most of its time on a concrete in-depth application of `dagster-slurm` at ASCII (supply chain intelligence pipelines). [Metaxy](https://docs.metaxy.io/latest/) is introduced as a companion project for energy-aware incremental recompute — the virtual poster session covers it in depth.
